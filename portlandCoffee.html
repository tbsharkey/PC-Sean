America's premier coffee destination

Usually regarded as being in Seattle’s shadow, Portland has emerged in the past decade as a leader in coffee innovation. Home to many independent shops, roasters, and retailers among other service providers, Portland provides a unique and eclectic experience for anyone’s tastes.

We would like to highlight three of the more popular shops in town. Each is unique in presentation with offerings of multiple orgins using espresso, pourover, and drip methods.
