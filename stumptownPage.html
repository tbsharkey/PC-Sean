Considered by many to be Portland’s foray into third wave coffee, Stumptown Coffee has been making waves in Portland since 1999. Stumptown has four cafes in the city and several others in Seattle, Los Angeles, and New York. Noted most recently for the widespread use of cold brew coffee to include nitro injected, Stumptown’s products can be found at many independent cafes as well as on the shelves of grocery stores.
